Diverbo

My experience as a volunteer.

What I did ?

I spent two weeks in the south of Spain with Diverbo helping Spanish teenagers improve their english. We were put into teams and also had activites in the afternoon which gave us an opportunity to meet new people and get everyone to mix. I enjoyed my time at the camp I decided the following year to a new campsite and volunteer for another three weeks.



What I gained from the experience

Two weeks away from home with people I would have never known before the trip definitly put me outside my comfort zone and allowed me to become more outgoing. I also gained a lot of awarness as I was only 15/16 travelling at the time and getting through Dublin and Madrid airports to me was an achievement.
