My Interests

What I did

When I was 16 I spent two months in the small town of Chevreuse just 30 mins south of Paris.I stayed with a french family and during my stay I got the opportunity to attend secondary school, gain work experience in a local primary school and bakery and also 'au-pair' for a period of time. I attended the secondary school Lycee De La Valle De Chevreuse to continue my learning and to meet friends my age which I am still in contact with to this day.





Why?

I enjoy stepping outside my comfort zone and this experience was definitly difficult but also very rewarding. As I was only 16 at the time I was in way near being fluent in the language but with hard work I improved slowly over the two months. I've always loved travelling and traveling solo was also rewarding, I've become more aware and capabale of finding solutions fast.
